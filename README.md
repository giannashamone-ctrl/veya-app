# 🌌 Veya — Timing Meets the Right Support

**Veya is a platform that helps people navigate life transitions by connecting them to the right support at the right time.**

By combining personal birth data with predictive timing insights, Veya translates upcoming life phases into clear guidance — and directly connects users to coaches, healers, and facilitators who can support them through it.

---

## 🚀 The Problem

People go through intense and often confusing life phases — such as:

- Breakups or relationship shifts  
- Career changes or identity crises  
- Periods of rapid expansion or opportunity  

During these times, most people don’t know:

- *What is happening to them*  
- *Why it is happening now*  
- *How to navigate it effectively*  
- *Who can actually help them*  

As a result, they feel lost, overwhelmed, and unsupported.

---

## 💡 The Solution

**Veya transforms life timing into clarity and connection.**

Users input their birth data and instantly receive a personalized overview of their upcoming life phases over the next 12 months.

As new phases approach, Veya:

- Notifies users with clear, human-readable insights  
- Explains the emotional and practical themes of that phase  
- Recommends relevant support options  
- Connects them directly to trusted facilitators  

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 🧾 Personalized Onboarding
- Sign up via email/password or Google  
- Enter birth date, time, and location  

### 📅 12-Month Life Phase Overview
- Clear timeline of upcoming transitions  
- Highlights both challenges and opportunities  

### 🔔 Smart Notifications
- Alerts users when a new phase is approaching  
- Provides context on what to expect and how to prepare  

### 🤝 Facilitator Matching
- Connects users with coaches, healers, and guides  
- Recommendations based on current life phase  

### 🛍 Offer Marketplace
- Browse sessions, programs, and experiences  
- Direct booking or external links  

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. User signs up and enters birth data  
2. System generates a personalized timeline of upcoming phases  
3. Each phase is translated into:
   - Emotional themes  
   - Potential challenges  
   - Growth opportunities  
4. Matching system suggests relevant support providers  
5. User connects, books, or explores offers
